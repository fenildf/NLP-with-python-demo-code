<desc>The &quot;jump unit&quot; is the informal name we have given to the collection of propulsion and flight-control systems that work together to allow small alien units vertical movements. The fundamentals are not particularly complex; the British-built Harrier jump-jet demonstrated them more than a decade ago. Scaling them down to the point where they can be integrated into personal armour, on the other hand, is.</desc>
